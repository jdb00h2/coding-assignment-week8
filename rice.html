Honey Garlic Chicken Stir Fry

Original recipe can be located through this link: Fried Rice

Prep Time 5 minutes
Cook Time 10 minutes
Total Time 15 minutes
Servings 4-6
Author Ali Martin

Ingredient List

Ingredient Quantity
Butter 3 T, divided
Eggs 2, whisked
Medium Carrots 2, peeled and diced
Frozen Peas 1/2 Cup
Garlic 3 Cloves, minced
Salt & Black Pepper To taste
Rice 4 Cups, cooked and chilled
Green Onions 3, thinly sliced
Soy Sauce 3-4 T
Oyster Sauce(optional) 2 tsps
Toasted Sesame Oil 1/2 tsp

Instructions

  1. Heat 1/2 tablespoon of butter in a large saute pan over meadium-high heat until melted.
  2. Add egg, and cook until scrambled, stirring occasionally.
  3. Remove egg, and transfer to a seperate plate.
  4. Add an additional 1 tablespoon butter to the pan and heat until melted.
  5. Add carrots, onion, peas and garlic, and season with a generous pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Saute for about 5 minutes or until the onion and carrots are soft.
  7. Increase heat to high, add in the remaining 1 1/2 tablespoons of butter, and stir until melted.
  8. Immediately add the rice, green onions, soy sauce and oyster sauce(if using), and stir until combined.
  9. Continue sauteing for an additional 3 minutes to fry the rice, stirring occasionally.
  10. Add in the eggs and stir to combine.
  11. Remove from heat, and stir in the sesame oil until combined.
  12. Taste and season with extra soy sauce, if needed.
  13. Serve immediately, or refrigerate in a sealed container for up to 3 days.
